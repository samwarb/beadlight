Beadlight

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

Beadlight is a Catholic prayer app designed to help users pray the Rosary with a calm, simple, and focused experience. This Privacy Policy explains what information Beadlight collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Beadlight is operated by Mercian Digital. You can contact us through the support page or by emailing beadlight@merciandigital.co.uk.

Summary

Beadlight does not require users to create an account to use the app. Beadlight does not sell personal information and does not use advertising tracking.

Beadlight may collect privacy-preserving prayer analytics so we can understand overall app usage, maintain aggregate prayer completion totals, improve reliability, and improve the prayer experience.

These prayer analytics may use a randomly generated app identifier stored on your device. This identifier is not your name, email address, Apple ID, advertising identifier, or payment information, but it may allow prayer events from the same app installation to be grouped together for reliability and aggregate counting.

Information We Collect

Prayer analytics and completion counts

When users move through or complete prayers in Beadlight, the app may send prayer analytics events to our backend. This helps us maintain an overall count of prayers completed in the app and understand how the prayer experience is being used.

These analytics may include:

  • when a prayer step is completed;
  • when a decade is completed;
  • when a Rosary is completed;
  • the selected mystery set, such as Joyful, Luminous, Sorrowful, or Glorious;
  • the prayer type or prayer step;
  • the step number or decade number;
  • the date and time of the event;
  • the app version and build number;
  • the platform, such as iOS;
  • a randomly generated app identifier stored on your device;
  • a temporary app session identifier.

The randomly generated app identifier is used to help process analytics reliably and avoid duplicate counting. It is not intended to identify you directly. However, because it can group events from the same app installation, it should be understood as a privacy-preserving pseudonymous identifier rather than a direct personal identity.

Beadlight uses prayer analytics in aggregate. For example, we may display a total such as “1,000,000 prayers completed with Beadlight.” We do not sell this data, use it for advertising tracking, or use it to track you across other apps or websites.

App settings stored on your device

Beadlight may store app preferences locally on your device. This may include settings such as onboarding status, selected prayer options, selected voice, guided audio preferences, hands-free settings, background sound preferences, and other app settings.

These settings are used to make the app work properly and provide a consistent prayer experience. They are stored locally on your device unless you delete or reset the app.

Guided audio and background sounds

Beadlight may include guided prayer audio and background sound features. The app does not record your voice, listen to your prayers, or access your microphone.

Audio features are used only to play prayer guidance and background sounds selected within the app.

Support requests and suggestions

If you contact us through the support form, we collect the information you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, ticket type, priority, subject, message, and device or app details.

We use this information to respond to your request, investigate bugs, consider suggestions, and improve Beadlight. We may also send you an acknowledgement email confirming that your support request has been received.

Roadmap administration

Beadlight’s roadmap admin page is restricted to approved administrator email addresses. If you are an approved administrator, your email address may be used to authenticate access to the roadmap management page.

In-app purchases and subscriptions

Beadlight may offer optional paid features, including guided audio access, through Apple In-App Purchase and subscriptions.

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. Beadlight does not receive or store your full payment card details. You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.

What We Do Not Collect

Beadlight does not intentionally collect:

  • your precise location;
  • your contacts;
  • your photos;
  • your microphone recordings;
  • your camera data;
  • your full payment card details;
  • advertising identifiers for tracking.

Beadlight does not listen to your prayers, record your voice, or access your microphone for prayer tracking.

How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • to provide and improve Beadlight;
  • to maintain an aggregate count of prayers completed;
  • to understand which prayer features are being used at an aggregate level;
  • to reduce duplicate counting and improve analytics reliability;
  • to respond to support requests and suggestions;
  • to manage the public roadmap and admin access;
  • to operate subscriptions and purchases through Apple.

Lawful Basis for Processing

Where UK GDPR applies, we rely on legitimate interests for limited, privacy-preserving product analytics such as prayer completion counts, app reliability, and product improvement.

For support requests, we process the information you provide so we can respond to your message and resolve the issue or suggestion you submitted.

We aim to minimise the amount of information collected and avoid collecting information that directly identifies a user unless it is needed, such as when someone submits a support request.

Anonymous, Pseudonymous, and Aggregate Data

Beadlight’s prayer analytics are intended to be privacy-preserving and used in aggregate. However, because the app may use a randomly generated app identifier to group events from the same installation, this data may be considered pseudonymous rather than fully anonymous.

The ICO distinguishes anonymised information from pseudonymised information. Pseudonymisation can help reduce privacy risk, but pseudonymised data may still be personal data depending on context. Beadlight does not intend to display individual user prayer histories publicly or associate prayer completion counts with a named individual.

Data Sharing

Beadlight does not sell personal information.

We may use trusted service providers to operate Beadlight and its website, including:

  • Apple, for App Store distribution, purchases, and subscriptions;
  • Supabase, for backend features such as prayer analytics, roadmap data, and support form handling;
  • Resend, for sending support ticket emails and acknowledgement emails;
  • GitHub Pages, for website hosting.

These providers process information only as needed to provide their services to Beadlight.

Data Retention

Prayer analytics may be retained so we can maintain historical aggregate totals, reduce duplicate counting, understand overall app usage, and improve Beadlight.

Support tickets and related emails may be retained for customer support, bug investigation, and record-keeping purposes.

Local app settings remain on your device unless you delete or reset the app.

Your Choices

Basic prayer completion analytics are part of how Beadlight maintains aggregate prayer counts and improves the app. These analytics are designed to be privacy-preserving and are not used for advertising tracking.

You can delete the app from your device to stop future app analytics from being sent. Deleting or reinstalling the app may also reset locally stored app preferences and identifiers.

Children’s Privacy

Beadlight is a general prayer app. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support request, contact us and we will review or delete it where appropriate.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of personal information we hold about you.

Because Beadlight’s prayer analytics are not linked to a user account, name, or email address, we may not be able to locate or delete a specific individual’s prayer analytics events.

If you have contacted support and want us to delete your support request, email us at beadlight@merciandigital.co.uk.
