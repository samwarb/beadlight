Account deletion

Request deletion of your Beadlight account

Beadlight users can ask for their app account and associated Beadlight data to be deleted. Use the support form below and choose Account deletion, or email support@merciandigital.co.uk.

What to include

  • The email address used for your Beadlight account.
  • A clear request to delete your Beadlight account and associated data.
  • Any details that help us identify the account if your current email is different.

What is deleted or retained

We delete your Beadlight account and Beadlight-owned cloud data linked to that account, including account-linked prayer journey records where applicable. We may retain limited records when required for security, fraud prevention, purchase verification, accounting, tax, legal obligations, or to keep a record of the deletion request. Anonymous or aggregate prayer analytics that are not linked to your account may not be identifiable for individual deletion.

Account deletion does not cancel Apple or Google Play subscriptions. Please manage active subscriptions through your app store subscription settings. We aim to review deletion requests as soon as reasonably possible, normally within 30 days.
